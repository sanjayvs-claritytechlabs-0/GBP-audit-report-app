Orthodontic treatment for Adults

Orthodontic Appliances in Napa & Petaluma, California

Searching for a “good orthodontist near me” for braces removal or orthodontic appliances treatment? You are in luck, we offer a large selection of orthodontic appliances, such as:

Palate Expanders

A palatal expander (RPE) applies gentle, consistent pressure to gradually widen the upper jaw by stimulating new bone growth. It consists of two metal parts connected by a screw and is attached to the upper teeth and molars.

Teeth Retainers

Retainers keep your teeth in their new position after braces. They prevent shifting by helping your gums, bones, and muscles adjust to the change.

Herbst and MARPE Appliance

The Herbst Appliance corrects severe overbites by guiding the lower jaw forward and upper molars back. Worn with braces, it works continuously and comfortably for effective, long-term results.

A MARPE device widens a narrow upper jaw by applying expansion forces directly to bone using miniscrews for anchorage. Unlike traditional expanders, it minimizes dental side effects while improving jaw width, airway volume, and breathing in both teens and adults.

TMJ Mouth Guards

TMJ mouth guards, or night guards, help relieve TMJ symptoms and protect teeth from grinding or bruxism. They can reduce jaw discomfort, prevent wear, and protect against tooth fractures over time.
